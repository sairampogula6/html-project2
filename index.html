About Director

S. S. Rajamouli, born in 1973, is an acclaimed Indian film director and screenwriter celebrated for his grand storytelling and visual brilliance. He began his directorial career in 2001 with Student No.1 and went on to deliver several blockbusters like Magadheera, Eega, and the globally acclaimed Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) and Baahubali: The Conclusion (2017). Known for blending mythology, emotion, and cutting-edge technology, Rajamouli has redefined Indian cinema on a global scale. His exceptional filmmaking has earned him numerous national and international awards, including a Golden Globe and an Oscar nomination for RRR (2022). With strong characters, emotional depth, and stunning visuals, his films continue to captivate audiences worldwide. Rajamouli remains an inspiration for a new generation of filmmakers with his creativity, vision, and dedication to cinematic excellence.
